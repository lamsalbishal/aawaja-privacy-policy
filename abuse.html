Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

We are committed to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform. Any attempt to share, promote, or store such content is strictly prohibited and will be dealt with immediately.

Zero-Tolerance Policy

Any user posting CSAE content will be permanently banned. We will report all such activity to global law enforcement agencies and NCMEC (National Center for Missing & Exploited Children).

Moderation and Detection

  • All content is monitored using AI-based scanning (e.g., Google Cloud Vision, Microsoft Content Moderator).
  • Manual moderation is used for flagged content.

Reporting Abuse

If you see any harmful or CSAE-related content, report it immediately via our in-app report feature or email: abuse@yourdomain.com.

Cooperation with Authorities

We cooperate with local and international law enforcement to support investigations into CSAE cases.

Our mission is to maintain a safe, respectful environment for all users.
